How to Book with us

To join as a club member, please follow these steps:

Fill out the form below to send an email to Club Maunga. Look out for our reply email, which will contain further instructions.
Send us the following required information to confirm your membership!:
- Full name
- Physical address
- Proof of address (e.g., copy of power bill)
- Proof of ID (driver's licence or passport)
For any inquiries, contact us at: maungaclub@maungaclub.maungaclub

Your membership will be confirmed via email, and additional details about the Club and accommodations will be sent to you by regular mail.
